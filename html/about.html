About the Project

This guide helps you explore and match with Stellaris multiplayer communities based on your preferences. It also aids Stellaris communities to represent themselves, find more users, and discover potential for collaboration with other groups.

Features:

  • Browse and filter communities by detailed categories
  • See descriptions and explanations for each category
  • Find your best community matches
  • Communities can submit their group for inclusion
Credits:
  • Created by eirish, GRP Community
Instructions:
  • Use the Matching tab to select your preferences
  • Switch to Communities to browse all available groups
  • Click Show Ranking to see your best matches
  • Communities can use the Submit Community tab to add their group
